Solar Plexus ChakraPersonal Power, Action, Decision and Identity

The Solar Plexus is the engine of action. It governs self-esteem, courage, decision-making capacity and the feeling of "I can." When it works well, you act with confidence. When it is dysregulated, either you cannot act — or you run over everything and everyone to prove you can.

👁The Mirror

What is happening with you

The Solar Plexus answers a central question: do you believe you can? Can you act, decide, take a stand, fail and get back up. When the Solar Plexus is healthy, there is a quiet "I can" that sustains everything. When it is dysregulated, that "I can" becomes "who am I?" (blocked) or "I am better than everyone" (excessive).

Blocked Solar Plexus in Blockage

When the Solar Plexus is blocked, personal power energy is suppressed. The person does not feel capable, worthy or deserving. There is a deep sense of inadequacy that contaminates all areas.

Typical Thoughts
  • "I am not good enough"
  • "Who am I to want this"
  • "If I take a stand, they will reject me"

What you feel

Deep insecurity Fear of judgment Chronic procrastination Imposter syndrome Difficulty saying no Excessive submission

The consequence: self-sabotage. The person has ideas, dreams, desires — but does not act. Not because they cannot, but because they do not believe they can. The blocked Solar Plexus creates a self-fulfilling prophecy: "I am not good enough" → does not act → no results → "see, I knew it."

Excessive Solar Plexus in Excess

When the Solar Plexus is in excess, personal power is dysregulated — too high. The person does not doubt themselves; they place themselves above others. Confidence becomes arrogance. Determination becomes aggressiveness.

Typical Thoughts
  • "I am better than others"
  • "Nobody can tell me what to do"
  • "Showing weakness is unacceptable"

What you feel

Defensive arrogance Anger when contradicted Destructive competitiveness Inability to ask for help Emotional coldness Contempt for "weakness"

The consequence: destroyed relationships. The person achieves results but pushes people away. They lead through fear, not respect. They win battles and lose bonds.

🔬The Formula

Why you work this way

Layer 1: The Species Code

In the human species, hierarchy is survival. In ancestral groups, those who positioned themselves too much without real power were expelled or eliminated. Those who positioned themselves too little were ignored and lost access to resources. The brain learned to constantly calibrate social status — and to avoid exposure when the risk of rejection is high.

"If I take a stand and fail, I could be rejected by the group" — this fear is not insecurity. It is a 300-thousand-year social survival program.

Layer 2: Your Story's Code

😤

Constant comparison

Grew up being compared with siblings, peers or impossible standards. The system recorded that it is never enough.

🚫

Punishment for taking a stand

Tried to take a stand and was ridiculed, silenced or punished. The system learned that being visible is dangerous.

🏆

Validation only through results

Only received love/attention when achieving something. The system learned that value = performance.

👊

Abusive power models

Saw power being used to control and dominate. The system either internalized the model (excess) or rejects any power (blockage).

The Positive Intention

💛

Solar Plexus blockage exists to protect you from rejection. If you do not expose yourself, you cannot be judged. Excess exists to ensure nobody dominates you. If you are the strongest, nobody can hurt you.

Both are armor. One protects by disappearing. The other protects by dominating. Neither is who you really are.

Why is it no longer efficient?

The armor that should protect you is imprisoning you:

  • Fear of being judged prevents you from being seen — and without being seen, nothing changes
  • The arrogance that should protect pushes away exactly the people who could support you
  • Self-sabotage confirms the belief of inadequacy in an infinite loop
  • The domination that guarantees power destroys trust and generates loneliness

⚡The Protocol

What to do when the pattern activates

Before

"I am not good enough" or "I am better than everyone"

→
After

"The Old Mind is activating the social status protocol. It is calibrating my position in the group — but with outdated data."

Part 1 — What's happening

"This is the social protection program. It wants to avoid rejection — but it is preventing me from acting."

Part 2 — What to do differently

"My worth does not depend on external approval. I can be imperfect and still deserve space."

If the Solar Plexus is blocked:

1

One stand per day: give an opinion in a conversation. Choose a restaurant. Say "I disagree." Start where the risk is low.

2

Capacity log: write down one thing you did well today. Not epic achievements — simple things. Recalibrate self-image with real evidence.

3

Say no once a week: to something small. The practice is not to be difficult — it is to prove to the system that limits do not cause catastrophe.

If the Solar Plexus is excessive:

1

Ask before asserting: "What do you think?" before imposing your view. The exercise is to listen, not to convince.

2

Admit one mistake per week: not as weakness — as demonstration of real strength. Those who admit mistakes gain more respect than those who never err.

3

Ask for help: deliberately, with something you could do alone. The exercise is to dismantle the self-sufficiency armor.

🔗Connections

Connections with other chakras

Solar Plexus Blocked→Root

The fear of acting in the Solar Plexus often needs the security of the Root as foundation. When you know you survive failure, it becomes easier to take risks.

"You can fail and stay standing."
Solar Plexus Excessive→Heart

Those who dominate without empathy need the Heart to humanize power. Leadership without heart becomes control.

"Power without love becomes control."
Throat Blocked→Solar Plexus

The fear of expressing yourself often comes from lack of self-esteem. The Solar Plexus provides the "I am worthy" foundation that allows the Throat to speak.

Crown→ Third Eye→ Solar Plexus For ideas that never become action
