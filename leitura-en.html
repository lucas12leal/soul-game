FAQ

Do I need to believe in chakras / energy?

No. The 7 centers work as a map of 7 dimensions of human experience (safety, pleasure, will, bonding, expression, vision, meaning). If you prefer to treat it as a psychological model, the reading works exactly the same. The map doesn't ask for faith. It asks for honesty.

Is this therapy?

No. It's diagnosis and navigation. Many people do the Reading alongside therapy, and bring the map to their therapist. If something shows up that calls for clinical care, I'll tell you so, with respect and clarity.

Is it religious?

No. The method has no doctrine, requires no religious practice, and doesn't conflict with your faith (or the absence of one).

What if no map resonates?

Then we open others. Your situation has more doors than the first three. And if there's still no recognition, the guarantee applies: your money back.

Why is there AI in the process?

The AI organizes the possible maps from the method's knowledge base. It decides nothing about you. In The Soul Game this is a constitutional rule: the tool offers hypotheses, your resonance chooses, your experience validates. It's the opposite of the digital oracle.

Does it work online?

The Reading was designed online: you need to see the screen (the maps and radar are visual). Zoom or Meet, recording available if you want it.
