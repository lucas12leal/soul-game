Introduction

The Structure of Soul Game

Life can often feel cruel, meaningless, chaotic, unfair — but it is none of those things. There is an explanation behind it that will make things make sense.

Every game has three fundamental elements: a goal, a scoring system, and a set of mechanics that define how to play.

In soccer the goal is simple: score more goals than the opponent. In life, however, the system is more complex. Soul Game does not have just one way to score. It has seven.

7
